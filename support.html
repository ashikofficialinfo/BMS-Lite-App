Common Questions

Why is the Chiller showing "OFF"? This indicates the main plant is powered down or in standby mode. Check the manual override in the Chiller tab.
How do I control Floor Levels? Only Admin users can toggle FCUs. Guest users have "Read Only" access to view temperatures.
System shows "Alarm"? Red alerts indicate a sensor failure or high-temperature threshold breach. Notify maintenance immediately.
